Target Belajar CeriaLingo SD/MI

Kelas 1 (Usia 6–7 tahun)

Fokus: Vocabulary dasar, phonics, dan kalimat sederhana

  • Alphabet, Greetings & Introduction
  • Numbers & Colors
  • Simple classroom objects
  • Contoh: “Hi, I am Rani.” / “This is a book.”
Kelas 2 (Usia 7–8 tahun)

Fokus: Vocabulary lanjutan, simple sentence structure

  • Food & Drinks
  • Family & Animals
  • Like / Don’t like
  • Contoh: “I like apples.” / “The giraffe is tall.”
Kelas 3 (Usia 8–9 tahun)

Fokus: Percakapan sederhana dan kalimat perkenalan

  • Hobbies & Daily Activities
  • Days of the Week
  • Short Dialogues & Questions
  • Contoh: “Do you like swimming?” / “I ride a bike on Sunday.”
Kelas 4 (Usia 9–10 tahun)

Fokus: Grammar dasar & aktivitas harian (Present Continuous)

  • Present Continuous “What are you doing?”
  • Describing Rooms/Things at Home
  • Math in English (There is / There are)
  • Contoh: “She is cooking.” / “There are 67 books.”
Kelas 5 (Usia 10–11 tahun)

Fokus: Deskripsi, perbandingan, dan ekspresi

  • Feelings & Clothes
  • Comparative Adjectives
  • Short Opinion Phrases
  • Contoh: “The giraffe is taller than the deer.” / “I’ve got stomachache.”
Kelas 6 (Usia 11–12 tahun)

Fokus: Teks sederhana, masa lalu & masa depan

  • Past Activities
  • Future Plans & Dreams
  • Telling Short Experiences
  • Contoh: “I studied last night.” / “I want to be a pilot.”

Alamat: Ds. Ngemplak RT 05 / 02 Mranggen Demak
