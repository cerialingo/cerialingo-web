Materi Program TOEFL

Basic & Advance - 12 Pertemuan

Materi Belajar Toefl 12 Pertemuan (3x / Minggu)

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 1: Be sure the sentence has a subject and a verb
• Skill 2: Be careful of object of prepositions
• Skill 3: Be careful of appositives
• Skill 4: Be careful of present participles
• Skill 5: Be careful of past participles

Reading:
• Skill 1: Answer main idea questions correctly
• Skill 2: Recognize the organization of ideas

Listening:
• Short Dialogues: Focus on the last line

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 6: Use coordinate connectors correctly
• Skill 7: Use adverb time and cause connectors correctly
• Skill 8: Use other adverb connectors correctly
• Skill 9: Use noun clause connectors correctly
• Skill 10: Use noun clause connector/subjects correctly

Reading:
• Skill 3: Answer stated detail questions correctly
• Skill 4: Find unstated details

Listening:
• Choose answers with synonyms
• Avoid similar sounds

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 11: Use adjective clause connector correctly
• Skill 12: Use adjective clause connector/subjects correctly
• Skill 13: Use reduced adjective clauses correctly
• Skill 14: Use reduced adverb clauses correctly
• Skill 15: Invert the subject and verb with question words

Reading:
• Skill 5: Find pronoun referents

Listening:
• Short Dialogues: Who, what, where — draw conclusions

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 16–20: Inverted subject-verb (place, negatives, conditionals, comparisons)
• Make verbs agree after prepositional phrases

Reading:
• Skill 6: Answer implied detail questions correctly

Listening:
• Listen for who/what in passive and with multiple nouns

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 21–25: Subject-verb agreement, parallel structure

Reading:
• Skill 7: Answer transitions questions correctly

Listening:
• Listen for negative and double negative expressions

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 26–30: Comparatives, superlatives, past participle after "have"

Reading:
• Skill 8: Find definitions from structural clues

Listening:
• Listen for almost negatives and comparatives

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 31–35: Tenses, participles, time expressions

Reading:
• Skill 9: Determine meanings from word parts

Listening:
• Listen for expressions of agreement

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 36–40: Tenses, passive form, countable/uncountable nouns

Reading:
• Skill 10: Use context to determine meanings of difficult words

Listening:
• Listen for uncertainty, suggestion, and surprise

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 41–45: Irregular/plural nouns, pronouns, references

Reading:
• Skill 11: Use context to determine meanings of simple words

Listening:
• Listen for contrary meaning, wishes, untrue conditions

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 46–50: Adjectives and adverbs usage

Reading:
• Skill 12: Determine where specific information is found

Listening:
• Listen for idiomatic expressions and multi-part verbs

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 51–55: Articles, general vs specific, -ed/-ing adjectives

Reading:
• Skill 13: Determine tone, purpose, or course

Listening:
• Long conversation: before and while listening

Grammar & Vocabulary:
• Skill 56–60: Prepositions, make vs do, like/alike/unlike, others

Reading:
• Skill 14: Determine where to insert a piece of information

Listening:
• Long talks: before and while listening
